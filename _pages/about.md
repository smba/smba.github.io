Welcome! I am a Ph.D. student at the software systems lab at Leipzig University in Germany. I earned both my B.Sc. and M.Sc. at TU Braunschweig, Germany with an undergraduate semester at University College Cork, Irelend and a six-month research internship at the Institute of Software Research at Canergie Mellon University.

My research explores the performance evolution of configurable software systems using methods ranging performance measurements, predictive modeling to transfer learning and code analysis techniques. My work is partly aligned with the research project 'Pervolution' in collaboration with Saarland University in Saarbrücken, Germany. My supervisor is Prof. Norbert Siegmund at Leipzig University.
